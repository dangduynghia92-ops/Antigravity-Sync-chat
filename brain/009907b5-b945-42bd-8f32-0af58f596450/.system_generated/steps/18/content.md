## [Basic Generation](https://docs.voicebox.sh/overview/generating-speech#basic-generation)
[Basic Generation](https://docs.voicebox.sh/overview/generating-speech#basic-generation)
Choose a voice profile from the dropdown
Type or paste your text
Click Generate and wait a few seconds
Preview and download the result

## [Text Formatting Tips](https://docs.voicebox.sh/overview/generating-speech#text-formatting-tips)
[Text Formatting Tips](https://docs.voicebox.sh/overview/generating-speech#text-formatting-tips)
The way you format text affects the output quality.

### [Punctuation](https://docs.voicebox.sh/overview/generating-speech#punctuation)
[Punctuation](https://docs.voicebox.sh/overview/generating-speech#punctuation)
Use proper punctuation for natural pauses:

```
Good: "Hello! How are you today? I'm doing great." Bad: "Hello how are you today Im doing great"
```


```
Good: "Hello! How are you today? I'm doing great." Bad: "Hello how are you today Im doing great"
```

### [Emphasis](https://docs.voicebox.sh/overview/generating-speech#emphasis)
[Emphasis](https://docs.voicebox.sh/overview/generating-speech#emphasis)
Use formatting to suggest emphasis:

```
- ALL CAPS for louder/emphasized: "That was AMAZING!" - Italics for subtle emphasis: "I *really* enjoyed that" - Bold for strong emphasis: "This is **very** important"
```


```
- ALL CAPS for louder/emphasized: "That was AMAZING!" - Italics for subtle emphasis: "I *really* enjoyed that" - Bold for strong emphasis: "This is **very** important"
```

The model interprets these hints but results may vary.
